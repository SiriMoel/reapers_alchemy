This mod requires [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3313573016]Souls[/url] to run.
Ensure this mod loads after Souls.

An expansion for Souls, adding a cauldron that can create items and upgrade wands using souls.
Adds a cauldron & spell to the room added by Souls.

[b]How do I use the cauldon?[/b]
Use the spell found in the cauldron to turn souls into Soul Idols.
Throw 3 Soul Idols into the cauldron and interact to create an item with effects depending on what souls were used.
Alternatively, throw in a wand and then 3 Soul Idols and interact to upgrade the wand's stats, based off what soul idols were used.
